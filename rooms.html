FAQ

What types of rooms do you offer?

We offer a variety of beautifully appointed rooms and suites designed with couples in mind. These include cozy standard rooms, luxurious suites with private balconies or patios, and sometimes even secluded bungalows or villas for ultimate privacy. Some of our rooms may also feature special amenities like jacuzzi tubs or fireplaces.

What amenities are included in each room?

Each of our rooms typically includes comfortable king or queen-sized beds, private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries, air conditioning and heating, flat-screen TVs, mini-fridges, coffee makers, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Depending on the room type, you may also find additional amenities such as robes and slippers, hair dryers, in-room safes, and irons and ironing boards.

Prices for the food

Our dining options vary, and so do the prices. We often offer all-inclusive packages that cover all meals and beverages. For guests not on these packages, we have restaurants with a range of price points. You can expect to find casual dining options with entrees in the $15-$30 range, while our more upscale restaurants may have entrees starting from $30 and up. We also have options for lighter fare and snacks at our cafes and bars. Please check our dining section for specific menus and pricing.

Amenities In-room

Beyond the standard amenities, some of our rooms offer special features to enhance your romantic getaway. These may include private balconies or patios with stunning views, jacuzzi tubs for relaxing soaks, cozy fireplaces, separate living areas, and upgraded sound systems. Please refer to the specific room descriptions when booking to see the full list of in-room amenities.
